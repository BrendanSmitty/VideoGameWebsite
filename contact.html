Split Screen Games Recommendations

Beat em ups!:

If you love versing your friends in a classic beat em up style game then these are my picks for some couch gameplay fun.

Tekken 7 is a fighting game developed and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It is the seventh main and ninth overall installment in the Tekken series. Tekken 7 was released to arcades in March 2015. This game has a large roster of fighters who have unique character traits and movesets, it is a lot of fun learning each characters unqiue fighting styles and battling against your friends. The game features multiple maps some of which have interactive parts where the players can smash their opponents through the walls or floors and the stage will progress. Tekken has been a top tier fighting game for a long time and this game is just another great installment in the franchise.

PlayStation All-Stars Battle Royale is a 2012 crossover fighting video game developed by SuperBot Entertainment, in conjunction with Bluepoint Games, and published by Sony Computer Entertainment. In this amazing brawling game you fight against your friends with beloved characters from various Playstation hits, play as your favourite playstation character and fight against your friends. This game is heavily inspired by Nintendo's Smash Bro's series, the only twist with this game is you don't knock the opponents off the maps to take off a life, instead by getting hits on them you fill a super bar, the bar can be used once filled once but the attack for level 1 is difficult to hit. If the super hits your opponent loses a life, however you can save up your super and fill the bar again to get level 2, and also once more for level 3, this is the last level and if you save up for level 3 you will land a devastating blow to your opponents.

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is a 2018 crossover fighting game developed by Bandai Namco Studios and Sora Ltd. and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch. It is the fifth installment in the Super Smash Bros. series, succeeding Super Smash Bros. for Nintendo 3DS and Wii U (2014). The game follows the series' traditional style of gameplay: controlling one of the various characters, players must use differing attacks to weaken their opponents and knock them out of an arena. In this installment of Smash their are many great new characters as well as the classic favourites, this game always gets intense when you and your friends are battling it out to see who's the king of Smash.

Racing games:

Racing games are some of the most fun games to play split screen with your buddies, here are my racing game recommendations.

MotorStorm: Apocalypse is a 2011 racing video game by Evolution Studios and published by Sony Computer Entertainment for the PlayStation 3. It is the fourth game in the MotorStorm series and the third for the PlayStation 3. Motorstorm has always been a super action packed racing game, but apocalypse takes it to another level. The maps in this game have crazy environments, the track will continuely change as buildings collapse, tornado's hit, and so on. This game is racing with pure chaos and your friends are right there with you as you race each other trying to survive the onslaught.

Mario Kart 8 is a 2014 kart racing game developed and published by Nintendo for the Wii U. It retains the gameplay of previous games in the Mario Kart series, with players controlling a Mario franchise character in races around tracks. Tracks are themed around locales from the Super Mario platform series and are populated with power-ups that help players gain advantages in races. Different difficulties are selectable prior to a race; harder difficulties make gameplay faster. In the new anti-gravity sequences, players drive on walls and ceilings. Mario Kart 8 contains a variety of single-player and local and online multiplayer games modes, including Grand Prix racing and arena-based battle modes.

Working together games:

Sometimes the funniest moments are when you try to work together with your team-mate for some common goal, here are some great games that make you work together.

LittleBigPlanet (LBP) is a puzzle platform video game series created and produced by British developer Media Molecule and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. Most games in the series put a strong emphasis on user-generated content and are based on the series' tagline "Play, Create, Share". The tagline represents the three core elements of the series: playing alone or with others locally (on the same console) or online, creating new content using the in-game creation tools, and sharing creations and discoveries online with other players. This game is amazing in many ways, the fact that players can create their own levels and share them to the public so that anyone can jump in and try players whacky levels, it is a lot of fun with friends as the game encourages team work.

Overcooked 2 is a cooperative cooking simulation game, which encourages team work to complete orders as you work in a crazy resturant. The game features many levels which increasing difficulty as it throws more and more at the players and forces you to adapt and improve quickly in order to be able to complete tasks on time. This game is a lot of fun with friends as you shout at each other trying desperatly to commmunicate and be as efficient as possible but most of the time it all comes apart. You'll be laughing as you fail miserably in some parts, whilst rejoicing in triumph once you finally click with your buddies and work together like a well oiled clock.
