My Personal Top 10 Games

1.

Infamous Second Son is a 2014 action-adventure game developed by Sucker Punch Productions and published by Sony Computer Entertainment for the PlayStation 4. The standalone sequel to the 2011 video game Infamous 2 and the third installment in the Infamous series, it was released worldwide on March 21, 2014. You play as Delsin Rowe, a conduit who can control multiple elemtal based super powers as you defeat enemies in this third person open world game set in Seattle.

2.

Uncharted 4: A Thief's End is a 2016 action-adventure game developed by Naughty Dog and published by Sony Computer Entertainment. It is the fourth main entry in the Uncharted series. You play as Nathan Drake, a man obbessed with finding historical treasures and goes to great lengths to discover them. This game has first person shooting combined with third person parkour sequences, in this fourth installment we learn about Nathan's brother Samuel.

3.

Rocket League is a vehicular soccer video game developed and published by Psyonix. The game was first released for PlayStation 4 and Windows in July 2015, with ports for Xbox One and Nintendo Switch being released later on. This game is an Esport, with a very high skill base, the average player has about 500 hours and is a very competitive skill based game. Rocket League has a few game modes, the standard car soccer mode, as well as hoops, snowday, rumble, and so on.

4.

The Last of Us is an action-adventure survival horror game franchise created by Naughty Dog and Sony Interactive Entertainment. The series is set in a post-apocalyptic United States ravaged by cannibalistic creatures infected by a mutated fungus in the genus Cordyceps. Similar to zombies this horrer game tests your survival skills whilst delivering one of the most chilling and amazing stories ever told in a video game.

5.

Elden Ring is a 2022 action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware. It was directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki with worldbuilding provided by fantasy writer George R. R. This game pushes the limits of souls games as it pushes you into a huge open world map, with seemingly endless number of enemies, bosses, weapons, and other secret items and areas this has to be the best souls game that has been made.

6.

Persona 5 is a 2016 role-playing video game developed by P-Studio and published by Atlus. It is the sixth installment in the Persona series, itself a part of the larger Megami Tensei franchise. Persona is a turn based fighting game, filled with fantasy monsters and just like in all previous persona games you follow a group of highschool kids as they grow closer as friends and take on the various evils in the world. You visit an alternative reality where the group can control personas to battle monsters and restore the hearts of twisted people.

7.

Final Fantasy XV is an action role-playing game developed and published by Square Enix. The fifteenth main installment of the Final Fantasy series, it was released for the PlayStation 4 and Xbox One in 2016, Windows in 2018, and as a launch title for Stadia in 2019. This game has a big main map, with other large areas that you can visit throughout the story of the game. In Final Fantasy 15 you place as a prince Noctis, he has a gang of 3 others who accompany him on his journey through this heavily fantasy land, battle monsters with spells and swords and live out your fantasy world dreams.

8.

Ghost of Tsushima is a 2020 action-adventure game developed by Sucker Punch Productions and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. The player controls Jin Sakai, a samurai on a quest to protect Tsushima Island during the first Mongol invasion of Japan. Jin learns the ways of stealth as he decides he will abandon honor as the mongols do not fight with honor. Jin uses both samurai style and ninja style to defeat his enemies, he also learn many techniques along the way such as bow and arrow, and ninja weapons like throwing stars, smoke bombs and more. This game tells a great story about a hero who must do what it takes to defend and save his island from total destruction from the mongol army.

9.

In Minecraft, players explore a blocky, procedurally generated, three-dimensional world with virtually infinite terrain and may discover and extract raw materials, craft tools and items, and build structures, earthworks, and machines. Depending on their chosen game mode, players can fight hostile mobs, as well as cooperate with or compete against other players in the same world. Minecraft has received critical acclaim, winning several awards and later being cited as one of the greatest video games ever created.

10.

Borderlands is an action role-playing first-person looter shooter video game franchise set in a space Western science fiction setting, created and produced by Gearbox Software and published by 2K for multiple platforms. This cartoon style first person shooter is a refreshing take on the genre, with loveable characters and a tonne of guns to loot, this game is has got to be regarded as one of the most fun shooter games. Another great part of borderlands is it's co-op feature, as playing through the game with friends is one of the most fun I've had in multiplayer.
