Multiplayer Games Recommendations

Esports:

If you have a competitive edge and want to prove you're the best in games, then these are right up your alley.

Rocket League is a vehicular soccer video game developed and published by Psyonix. The game was first released for PlayStation 4 and Windows in July 2015, with ports for Xbox One and Nintendo Switch being released later on. This game is an Esport, with a very high skill base, the average player has about 500 hours and is a very competitive skill based game. Rocket League has a few game modes, the standard car soccer mode, as well as hoops, snowday, rumble, and so on.

Overwatch was a 2016 team-based multiplayer first-person shooter game by Blizzard Entertainment. The game was first released for PlayStation 4, Windows, and Xbox One in May 2016 and Nintendo Switch in October 2019. Cross-platform play was supported across all platforms. Choose between a roster of unique heros and master their moveset to lead your team to victory, overwatch promises for some competitive fun and the characters all have their own quirks.

Shooter multiplayer games:

Everyone loves online multiplayer shooters, here are my picks for some shooter fun with friends.

Fortnite is an online video game developed by Epic Games and released in 2017. In this game you are in a big battle royal, this is where a big group of players are all thrown into a big open world map and forced to battle it out until theres only 1 left standing and is crowned the winner. Fortnite has become one of the most popular games of all time and is known for its addictive gameplay, many play with friends as you can be the last team standing playing in either duos or trios. You get dropped in with nothing on you and you have to loot around to get good equipment so you can defeat the other players and be the last to survive.

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive is a 2012 multiplayer tactical first-person shooter developed by Valve and Hidden Path Entertainment. It is the fourth game in the Counter-Strike series. CSGO offers a competitive classic war style shooter game that is best experienced with other players. With the voice chat feature CSGO has many funny moments, as well as offering other modes such as surf, where you can compete against your friends to reach the end of ellaborate courses the fastest. Theres a reason this game has been on top for so long, easy to jump in with friends and have a great time.

Party games:

Here are some other fun online multiplayer games

Portal 2 is a 2011 puzzle-platform video game developed by Valve for Windows, Mac OS X, Linux, PlayStation 3, and Xbox 360. The digital PC version is distributed online by Valve's Steam service, while all retail editions were distributed by Electronic Arts. In portal 2 you and a friend must work together to solve some very unique and challenging puzzles, the game mostly relys on the portal creating mechanic and the clever ways it can be used to solve puzzles. This game is a lot of fun and because you and your buddy are relying on each other it allows for a lot of pranking potential and laughs of course.

In Minecraft, players explore a blocky, procedurally generated, three-dimensional world with virtually infinite terrain and may discover and extract raw materials, craft tools and items, and build structures, earthworks, and machines. Depending on their chosen game mode, players can fight hostile mobs, as well as cooperate with or compete against other players in the same world. Minecraft has received critical acclaim, winning several awards and later being cited as one of the greatest video games ever created.

Random Gaming Fact!

Fact No.3: Minecraft is the Best-Selling Game Ever

Minecraft is currently the best-selling game ever. Its creator Marcus Alexej Persson (a.k.a Notch), recorded over $180 million in sales. It also remains the most played game across all platforms.
